Who we are

Timber packaging and cargo preparation from Boksburg.

JP Crating & Packing supports customers who need reliable timber crates, protective packing, strapping and load preparation.

Based in Boksburg, South Africa, the business presents a practical service offering for customers who need packaging that is built around the item, ready for transport, and easier to handle on site.

Approach

Fit-for-purpose builds

Crates and skids are sized around the equipment and the way it needs to move.

Protection

Internal cushioning and support

Foam and bracing help reduce shifting, impact and contact damage during transport.

Handling

Prepared for loading

Items can be strapped, braced and readied for dispatch or container loading.
